coming March 31st and April 1st starting at 9AM each day to the Davis Conference Center in Layton is SpiritCon.

This is a non-denominational convention for everyone who wants to learn more about energy in a number of different ways; new, experienced, or just curious. Join us for two days brimming with classes and panels on energy, personal spiritual growth, divination, traditions, classes for kids, and much more.

Besides all of the amazing presentations we will also have panels that will include:

What is a Witch/Witchcrafting, Comparative Systems in Paganism, How to Support your Witch, Community for Solitaries, Safety (Warding, Physical, Etc.), Spell Writing, Meditation, and Ritual Construction.

Classes that will be offered at our two-day event

  • Craft classes include Everyday Crafting, Flat Magic (Paper Crafts), Altars, and Crystal Grids.
  • Energy Classes include Empathic Defense, Chi Gung, Chakras, and Dance Chant Magic.
  • Nature classes include Animals (Totems and More), Plants, Rocks/Crystals, and Mythical Creatures.
  • Cycles classes include Wheel of the Year, Lunar, Mysteries, and Places of Power.
  • Traditions classes include Greek/Roman, Celtic, Egyptian, Norse, Root Magic, Intro to Qabala, and Beginning Devotional Practices.
  • Divination classes include Tarot reading, Tea Leaf reading, Casting bones, Astrology, Runes, Pendulum, and Sigils.

We also have some wonderful events for the children!

Kids Classes will be broken into three age groups 3 to 7 (Sprites), 8 to 13 (Leprechaun), 14 to 17 (Goblins). Each age group will go over a variant of the following classes: Herbs, Moon Phases, Crystals, Tools, Grounding, and Animal Spirits.

We will have a vendor room full of crystals, wood burned items, hand tooled leather works, jewelry, hand made crafts, soaps, candles, and more to aid in your spiritual journey.

There will also be a reader and healer room for Tarot, Medium, and Psychic readings, as well as Reiki and Pranic Healing, and a wonderful massage therapist.
